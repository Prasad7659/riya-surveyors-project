About us

Riya Surveyors, established in 2018 and located in Yapral, Hyderabad, Telangana, is a trusted provider of high-quality engineering and surveying solutions. With a strong focus on research, technical expertise, and attention to detail, the firm delivers comprehensive support from project initiation to completion. Their services cover a wide range of specialties, including land surveys, transmission line assessments, pipeline mapping, and road surveys. Riya Surveyors is known for building long-term client relationships through customized, reliable, and efficient solutions. Leveraging advanced geodetic technologies, the team ensures precise results across various engineering and infrastructure projects.
